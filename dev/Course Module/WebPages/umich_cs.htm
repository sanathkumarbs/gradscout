Course offerings

Browse or search the catalog of UMSI course offerings below. Courses offered in the past two years and new courses planned for an upcoming term are included. Click on the course number or title to view a brief description, credit hours, advisory or required prerequisites (if any), and a link to a recent syllabus.

For term schedule information for a current or upcoming semester, including instructor name, day, time, and location, and to register for courses, visit Wolverine Access (under Public Resources, select U-M Course Catalog, then Class Search by School/College, then Information).

See links at left to review descriptions of courses indicated as "special topics" and information on internships for credit, dissertation/candidacy credit, and independent study courses.
